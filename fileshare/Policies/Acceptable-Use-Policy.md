# Acceptable Use Policy (v3.2)
Effective: 2026-01-01 | Owner: IT Security

1. Company systems are for business use. Limited personal use is permitted
   where it does not interfere with duties or violate this policy.
2. Credentials must never be shared. MFA is mandatory on all remote access.
3. Customer data may only be stored in approved systems (CRM, M365 tenant,
   the corporate file share). Personal cloud storage is prohibited.
4. AI tools may only access company data through the approved AI gateway.
   Direct connection of AI clients to internal systems is prohibited.
5. Violations are handled per the HR disciplinary matrix (HR-POL-007).
